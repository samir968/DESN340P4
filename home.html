Volunteering at RASC Edmonton Centre

Posted on May 31, 2025

All the amazing work accomplished by the RASC Edmonton Centre is done by volunteers.

If you are a member and would like to contribute your time and talents to helping the RASC EC achieve its mandate, go to the Members’ Area and click on the Volunteers tab for more details.

You’ll find a page just for volunteers with links to the volunteer self-registration form, and announcements about current Club events and activities requiring volunteer help.
