RASC Regular Meeting

In-person! Note 1 day postponement to Thu Oct 30, 7:30-11 pm. Bring your scope/binocs/eyes. We’ll bring extra eyepieces.

Bring your scope, we’ll teach you how to use it. As always, overdress, standing around one chills surprisingly quickly. Keep an eye out for cancellation on Facebook and the email list.  Location: Chickakoo Lake (west of the city, Northwest of Spruce) https://maps.app.goo.gl/2px2NdtskwFZyWDY6

Future zoom session: Nov 19, Dec 17. [ 2025 ] (Generally 3rd Wed) 

Please join us (via Zoom) once a month for a chit-chat (what have you seen recently? Any skygazing plans for the upcoming month? Q&A about telescopes and visual astronomy). What observing/scope topics would you like to discuss? Let us know. Your hosts are Murray, Berta, and Alister. As Earth completes a near circle around the Sun, we will cover a range of topics that will help you earn the ‘Explore the Universe’ certificate and pin from the Royal Astronomical Society of Canada. These include:

  • Finding your way around the sky
  • Moon, Sun, and planet cycles
  • Eyes, binoculars, telescope basics
  • Star clusters, craters on the Moon, comets
  • Using the wonderful RASC Observer’s Handbook
