About Stardust

Stardust is the newsletter of the RASC Edmonton Centre, published ten times per year, January to June and September to December. Subscription is automatic with membership in the Centre. Content is contributed by members. The newsletter is available online a few days before Regular Meetings.

Please submit articles by email to the Stardust Editor. Accepted formats are Microsoft Word (.doc), plain text (.txt), Rich Text Format (.RTF), Open Office (.sxw) or Open Document (.odt).

NOTE: The June 2013 issue was the last one published on paper. As of September 2013, Stardust is available only in digital format.
